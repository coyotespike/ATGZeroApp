Instructions:

  1. Click "Download 192x192" to save the small icon
  2. Click "Download 512x512" to save the large icon
  3. Place both PNG files in the same directory as index.html
  4. The PWA is now ready to install!

How to Install:

On iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open the website in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" - the app icon will appear on home screen

On Android:

  1. Open the website in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu (three dots)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" - the app icon will appear on home screen
